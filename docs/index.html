Introduction

This is a list of R resources, links and tips that is being curated to help particularly with training on the Tidyverse and working efficiently in R. A good place to get started with learning about R is the PHI Introduction to R in NSS. If you want to try out R without installing it, you can do this on RStudio Cloud.

For the tidyverse section, I provide a recommended order to work through materials for training.
